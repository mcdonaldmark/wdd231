Site Plan

 

Site Name

A Little Taste of Ecuador - We chose this name to provide a little bit of Ecuadorian cuisine and add a litte more diversity in Utah.

 

Site Purpose

For A Little Taste of Ecuador:

  • The website serves to provide another venue of cultural diversity by creating a restaurant focused on selling traditional Ecuadorian dishes.
 

Scenarios

For A Little Taste of Ecuador:

  • What is the best dish to try?
  • Are the prices reasonable?
  • Are the ingredients fresh?
  • Where do you source the ingredients?
  • Where and what way is the best to contact the owner?
